Yomon DAAB visit odatda bir xil boshlanadi: Board saytga keladi, tomonlar beton, quvur va grafiklarni ko'rsatadi, ikki soatdan keyin esa hech kim sanalar bo'yicha tartiblamagan claim haqida bahs boshlanadi.

Yaxshi DAAB site visit boshqacha ishlaydi. Board aniq project narrative oladi, asosiy issuesni ko'radi, qaysi records borligini tushunadi va masala formal referralga ketmasidan oldin yordam bera oladi.

## Site visit va formal referral farqi

[DAAB bo'yicha maqola](/uz/knowledge/daab-explained) dispute ladderni tushuntiradi: Engineer's determination, DAAB, Notice of Dissatisfaction, amicable settlement va arbitration.

Site visit bundan ertaroq va kengroq. Board faqat saytni ko'rmaydi; u project sequence, constraints va recurring issuesni tushuna boshlaydi.

Aralashtirmang:

- **dispute avoidance** - early discussion, risk areas, contract ruxsat bergan informal assistance;
- **formal referral** - aniq dispute DAAB decision uchun protsessual yuboriladi.

Bu rejimlar aralashsa, site visit keraksiz legal submissionlarga to'ladi yoki aksincha, hech qanday foydali material bo'lmaydi.

## Tashrifdan oldin nima tayyorlanadi

DAABga 20 GB arxiv kerak emas. Boshqariladigan kontekst kerak.

### 1. Project snapshot

2-4 sahifalik orientation note:

- parties and contract form;
- current contract price va Time for Completion;
- commencement date va completion dates;
- current progress status;
- major milestones;
- key packages/subcontractors;
- critical site constraints;
- current high-risk issues.

Bu advocacy document emas. Bu orientation file.

### 2. Chronology

Har major issue uchun:

- date;
- event;
- instruction/notice/correspondence reference;
- affected work area or activity;
- current status.

Chronology zerikarli va tekshiriladigan bo'lishi kerak. Agar u ayblov nutqiga o'xshasa, Board ishonchi kamayadi.

### 3. Issue list

| Issue | Nega muhim | Current status | Board nimani tushunishi kerak |
|---|---|---|---|
| delayed access | sequence/critical pathga ta'sir | notices issued, mitigation ongoing | access specific activities bilan bog'liq |
| design approval delays | procurement/workni bloklaydi | submissions under review | approval workflow discipline kerak |
| variation valuation | payment/cash-flow risk | rates disputed | measurement trail muhim |
| ESHS incident | method/reportingga ta'sir | corrective action open | unrelated delaydan ajratish kerak |

Har issue dispute bo'lishi shart emas. Lekin har material issue tushunarli bo'lishi kerak.

### 4. Visual file

Photos faqat kontekst bo'lsa foydali:

- date and location;
- before/after;
- activity ID yoki work package;
- short caption;
- takroriy kadrlarsiz.

25 ta aniq foto 400 ta kontekstsiz suratdan kuchliroq.

### 5. Programme snapshots

Time issue bo'lsa, Boardga faqat percentage progress yetmaydi:

- baseline programme reference;
- last accepted/reviewed programme;
- current update;
- affected activities;
- critical yoki near-critical path explanation;
- mitigation/recovery actions.

Delay evidence haqida: [EOT va delay analysis](/uz/knowledge/eot-delay-analysis).

## Visit qanday o'tkaziladi

Amaliy format:

1. Joint opening meeting.
2. Neutral project update.
3. Agreed areas bo'yicha site walk.
4. Issue-by-issue discussion.
5. Kerakli recordsni aniqlash.
6. Action points summary.
7. Contract va DAAB rulesga mos minutes/agreed note.

Tomonlar oldindan qaysi masalalar informally muhokama qilinishi, qaysilari formal disputega yaqinligini kelishib olishi kerak.

## FAQ

### Har DAAB visit uchun legal submission kerakmi?

Odatda yo'q, agar routine yoki avoidance visit bo'lsa. Lekin sensitive issues legal team bilan oldindan ko'rilishi kerak.

### Visit packni kim tayyorlaydi?

Contract Manager, planner, QS, site team va legal support birga. Faqat yurist tayyorlasa facts zaif bo'lishi mumkin; faqat site team tayyorlasa contract logic zaif bo'ladi.

### Site visitda claims muhokama qilinadimi?

Ha, agar contract, DAAB rules va agreed meeting purpose bunga mos bo'lsa. Bu early discussionmi, informal assistancemi yoki formal dispute processmi - aniq bo'lishi kerak.
