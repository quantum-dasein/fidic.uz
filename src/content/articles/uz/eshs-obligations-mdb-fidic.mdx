Qurilish maydonida ESHS ko'pincha alohida dunyo kabi ko'riladi: alohida specialist, alohida reports, alohida meetings. Bu IPC bo'yicha savol, incident letter, community complaint yoki oxirgi uch oy recordsini zudlik bilan ko'rsatish talabi kelguncha davom etadi.

MDB-funded projectsda environmental, social, health and safety obligations "presentation uchun ESG" emas. Bu contract administrationning bir qismi. Ba'zan aynan ESHS payment certification, taking-over, claim defence yoki audit natijasini belgilaydi.

Asosiy xato - ESHSni faqat compliance function deb ko'rish. FIDIC/MDB loyihasida bu commercial risk ham.

## ESHS FIDIC loyihaga qayerdan kiradi

ESHS obligations bir necha hujjat qatlamida bo'ladi:

- procurement documents va Instructions to Bidders;
- Employer's Requirements;
- Particular Conditions;
- ESCP, ESMP, CESMP yoki o'xshash plans;
- method statements va site-specific plans;
- incident reporting procedures;
- monthly progress reports;
- payment va performance monitoring requirements.

Shuning uchun ESHS faqat technical specificationdan tekshirilmaydi. Butun tender package, jumladan [Section VIII va Contract Data](/uz/knowledge/world-bank-section-viii-contract-data/) bilan birga o'qiladi.

## Nega bu payment risk

Har bir ESHS finding avtomatik paymentni bloklamaydi. Bu contract wording, Particular Conditions, Engineer powers, ESCP/ESMP, procurement documents va applicable lawga bog'liq.

Lekin ESHS uch sababga ko'ra payment riskga aylanadi.

### 1. Supporting documents IPC file qismiga aylanadi

Contract ESHS reporting, permits, logs, training records, waste manifests, incident reports yoki grievance records talab qilsa, ularning yo'qligi reviewni kechiktiradi.

Muammo ish jismonan bajarilmaganida emas. Muammo contract va lender-supervised administration kutgan evidence yo'qligida.

### 2. Incident oddiy workflowni to'xtatadi

Serious accident, community complaint, environmental spill, labour issue yoki security incident immediate reporting va corrective action talab qilishi mumkin. Jamoa "kim aybdor" deb bahslashayotgan paytda works suspended, access restricted yoki approvals delayed bo'lishi mumkin.

Incident records zaif bo'lsa, keyin ESHS consequence ni ordinary delay yoki disruptiondan ajratish qiyin.

### 3. Audit trail va reputatsiya

World Bank ESF risk-based management, stakeholder engagement, grievance mechanisms va transparencyga tayanadi. Amalda bu project team qarorlari keyin tushuntirilishi kerak degani.

Records complaintdan keyin yig'ilgandek ko'rinsa, project controlsga ishonch pasayadi.

## Minimal ESHS administration map

Mobilisationdan oldin bitta map tuzing:

| Block | Nima aniq bo'lishi kerak |
|---|---|
| Obligations | qaysi ESHS duties contract documentsda |
| Owner | Contractor, Employer, Engineer, subcontractor, ESHS manager |
| Evidence | compliance qaysi records bilan isbotlanadi |
| Frequency | daily, weekly, monthly, immediate, event-based |
| Link to payment | IPC, milestone, taking-over yoki approval uchun nima kerak |
| Incident workflow | kimga, qanchalik tez, qaysi kanal bilan |
| Corrective action | non-conformity kim va qanday yopadi |
| Dispute risk | delay, variation, claim yoki deductionga qanday aylanishi mumkin |

Bu map bo'lmasa, ESHS tarqoq emails va messengers orqali yuradi. MDB loyihasi uchun bu zaif.

## Tenderdan oldin nima priced bo'lishi kerak

ESHS cost ko'pincha BoQ itemda emas, project organisation ichida yashaydi.

Tekshiring:

- dedicated ESHS staff kerakmi;
- commencementdan oldin qaysi plans kerak;
- mandatory trainings;
- monitoring and testing;
- camp management, labour influx, community liaison;
- reporting templates;
- SEA/SH, road safety, security personnel, biodiversity, chance finds;
- subcontract flow-down obligations;
- non-compliance consequences.

Bu akademik review emas. Bu price, programme va management capacity masalasi.

## Claims bilan bog'liqlik

ESHS issue claim sababi, claimga qarshi defence yoki claimdagi zaif joy bo'lishi mumkin:

- access delay unresolved community issue bilan bog'liq;
- incident investigationdan keyin works suspended;
- Engineer environmental controls sabab method statementni rad etadi;
- contractor EOT so'raydi, lekin o'z ESHS non-compliance delayga hissa qo'shgan;
- employer additional mitigation measures buyuradi va contractor uni variation deb ko'radi;
- payment dispute rejected ESHS documentation sabab chiqadi.

Har bir holatda dates, instructions, reports, corrective actions, photos, approvals, correspondence va programme impact kerak.

ESHS register [notice register](/uz/knowledge/notice-register-28-84-day-workflow/), variation register va programme updates bilan bog'langan bo'lishi kerak.

## FAQ

### Har qanday ESHS breach paymentni to'xtatadimi?

Yo'q. Oqibat contract, Particular Conditions, ESCP/ESMP, Engineer/Employer powers va breach xarakteriga bog'liq. Lekin zaif ESHS evidence pack reviewni cho'zishi mumkin.

### ESHS faqat contractor uchun muhimmi?

Yo'q. Employer/PIU ham risk allocation, approvals, stakeholder engagement, grievance mechanisms va lender reportingni boshqaradi.

### ESHS claims strategyga kiradimi?

Ha, lekin ehtiyotkor. ESHS records event, causation, mitigation va corrective actionsni ko'rsatadi; shu bilan birga contributory delay yoki non-complianceni ham ochishi mumkin.
