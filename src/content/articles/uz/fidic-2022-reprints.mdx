Tenderda tanish havola bor: FIDIC 2017. Keyin boshqa hujjatda "reprinted 2022 with amendments" yozuvi chiqadi. Yurist uchun bu editorial detail bo'lib ko'rinishi mumkin. Contract manager uchun esa bu risk: qaysi text priced qilingan, qaysi notice templates ishlatiladi, Particular Conditions qanday o'qiladi?

Bu maqola FIDIC matnini qayta aytmaydi va official publication o'rnini bosmaydi. Maqsad - tender documents FIDIC 2022 Reprintsga ishora qilganda amaliy tekshiruv yo'lini berish.

## Reprint nega muhim

FIDIC Red, Yellow va Silver Book second editionsni 2017-yilda chiqargan. Keyin 2022-yilda amendments bilan reprints paydo bo'lgan. FIDIC official pages bu versionsni alohida ko'rsatadi.

Loyiha uchun uch narsa muhim:

- base edition: 1999, 2017 yoki 2017 reprinted 2022;
- tender documents consistency: Letter of Tender, Contract Data, Particular Conditions, Employer's Requirements, securities forms;
- operational discipline: notices, claims, determinations, DAAB, payment cycle va records qaysi procedurega qarab yuradi.

Agar hujjatlar turli editionsni aralashtirsa, bu clarification questions ro'yxatiga kirishi kerak.

## Qayerda chalkashlik paydo bo'ladi

Muammo ko'pincha bitta aniq contradiction emas. U hujjatlar bo'ylab tarqaladi.

Instructions to Tenderers bitta formni aytadi. Contract Data boshqasini ko'rsatadi. Particular Conditions eski structurega yozilgan bo'lishi mumkin. Employer's Requirements notice templates ikkinchi edition logiciga mos kelmasligi mumkin. Jamoa training notesda hali FIDIC 1999 bilan yashaydi.

Natijada commercial team bitta version bo'yicha narx beradi, contract team ikkinchisi bo'yicha administratsiya qiladi, site team esa odat bo'yicha ishlaydi.

FIDIC formalar umumiy xaritasi uchun [Rainbow Series](/uz/knowledge/rainbow-guide), 1999 va 2017 farqlari uchun [FIDIC 1999 vs 2017](/uz/knowledge/fidic-1999-vs-2017)ni ko'ring.

## Pre-bid checklist

### 1. Exact formni yozib qo'ying

Tender review sheetda alohida qayd qiling:

- book colour: Red, Yellow yoki Silver;
- edition: Second Edition 2017;
- reprint 2022 with amendments bormi;
- language version;
- text source: official FIDIC publication, licensed copy yoki tenderga kiritilgan conditions.

Bu byurokratiya emas. Bu pricing errorni oldini oladi.

### 2. Contract Data va Particular Conditions

FIDIC 2017/2022da Contract Data va Special Provisions juda muhim. Aynan u yerda dates, notice addresses, securities, communication, payment va dispute board details konkretlashadi.

Savollar:

- barcha blanks to'ldirilganmi;
- clause references to'g'ri editiondanmi;
- amendments procedure balancega zarar qilmayaptimi;
- Contract Data, Employer's Requirements va forms mosmi;
- special conditions alohida price yoki allowance talab qiladimi.

World Bank loyihalarida bu [Section VIII va Contract Data](/uz/knowledge/world-bank-section-viii-contract-data) bilan bog'liq.

### 3. Notice workflow

FIDIC 2017/2022 ko'plab jamoalar o'rganmagan disciplined communication talab qiladi.

Tender stage:

- official channel qaysi;
- notices kim yuboradi;
- site events kim tutadi;
- notice register kim yuritadi;
- 28-day va 84-day milestones qanday kuzatiladi.

Awarddan keyin bu daily contract managementga aylanadi. Batafsil: [Notice Register under FIDIC 2017](/uz/knowledge/notice-register-28-84-day-workflow).

## FAQ

### FIDIC 2022 Reprints FIDIC 2017ni almashtirdimi?

Aniqroq aytganda, bu 2017 second editionsning amendments bilan reprintsidir. Har tenderda qaysi text incorporated qilinganini ko'rish kerak.

### Old notice templatesni o'zgartirish kerakmi?

Agar ular FIDIC 1999 yoki 2017/2022 procedures hisobga olinmagan eski projects uchun yozilgan bo'lsa, albatta review kerak.

### "FIDIC 2017" deb yozish yetarlimi?

Ba'zan hujjatlar shunday yozadi. Lekin international tenderda aniqlik yaxshiroq: agar reprinted 2022 with amendments nazarda tutilgan bo'lsa, contract documentsda ko'rinishi kerak.
