Ko'p pudratchilar MDB tenderlariga oddiy mantiq bilan yondashgan: qualificationdan o'tish, compliant bid topshirish va asosan narx bilan raqobat qilish. Merit Point Criteria bu odatni o'zgartiradi.

2026-yilda merit points qo'llanadigan ADB loyihalarida pudratchidan faqat past narx emas, balki ishonchli texnik taklif talab qilinadi: metodologiya, resurslar, risklarni boshqarish, sifat, ESHS compliance va realistik programme.

Bu maqola tender documents o'rnini bosmaydi. Har bir loyiha uchun Instructions to Bidders, Evaluation and Qualification Criteria, Particular Conditions va addenda hal qiluvchi ahamiyatga ega.

## Merit Point Criteria nimani o'zgartiradi

MPC texnik taklif sifatini kuchaytiradi. Narx muhimligicha qoladi, lekin u har doim yagona farqlantiruvchi omil bo'lmaydi.

Pudratchi quyidagilarni ko'rsatishi kerak:

- scope va risklarni tushunish;
- real construction methodology;
- ishonchli ish tashkiloti;
- key personnel malakasi;
- programme va sequencing sifati;
- environmental and social obligationsni boshqarish;
- quality assurance va health & safety yondashuvi;
- FIDIC kontraktini administratsiya qilish qobiliyati.

Zaif texnik taklif narx raqobatbardosh bo'lsa ham yutqazishi mumkin.

## Nega "avval narx, keyin ko'ramiz" xavfli

MDB FIDIC loyihalarida ko'p risklar tender bosqichidayoq ko'rinadi:

- Particular Conditions General Conditions balansini o'zgartiradi;
- ES obligations time and cost impact yaratadi;
- approvals va permits programmega ta'sir qiladi;
- interface risks metodologiya talab qiladi;
- Engineer procedures va reporting discipline resurs talab qiladi;
- payment and cash-flow provisions narxga ta'sir qiladi.

Tender team bularni ko'rmasa, muammo awarddan keyin contract team zimmasiga tushadi.

## Bid team qanday tuziladi

MPC uchun faqat estimator va commercial manager yetarli emas. Integratsiyalashgan jamoa kerak:

- planner;
- construction manager;
- contracts/claims specialist;
- quantity surveyor;
- HSE/ES specialist;
- procurement/logistics specialist;
- key subcontractor input;
- kerak bo'lsa local regulatory adviser.

Asosiy vazifa - technical proposal va price o'rtasida izchillik. Agar methodology night shifts, traffic management yoki additional supervision va'da qilsa, bu preliminaries va narxda aks etishi kerak.

## Tender documentsda nimani tekshirish kerak

Pre-bid tender risk review:

1. Evaluation criteria va weighting.
2. Minimum technical score.
3. Key personnel va equipment talablari.
4. Programme va methodology talablari.
5. FIDIC Particular Conditions.
6. ES requirements, site access va stakeholder constraints.
7. Payment terms, advance, retention, securities.
8. JV, subcontracting va local participation qoidalari.
9. Fraud and corruption / sanctions provisions.
10. Clarification deadline va addenda tracking.

Eng muhim nuqta - technical promises va commercial price mosligi.

## Texnik taklifni qanday yozish kerak

Yaxshi MPC taklifi chiroyli brochure emas. U tekshiriladigan project plan bo'lishi kerak.

Amaliy yondashuv:

- generic methodology emas, project-specific risksni ko'rsatish;
- programmeni resurslar bilan bog'lash;
- temporary works va access strategiyasini tushuntirish;
- interface managementni ko'rsatish;
- record keeping va contract administrationni yozish;
- claim prevention mindset qo'shish;
- key personnel FIDIC/MDB muhitini tushunishini ko'rsatish.

ADB va boshqa MDB tenderlarida keyin qo'shimcha xarajatsiz bajarib bo'lmaydigan va'dalardan qochish kerak.

## FAQ

### Har qanday narxda eng kuchli texnik taklif yozish kerakmi?

Yo'q. Taklif realistik bo'lishi va berilgan narxda bajarilishi kerak. Haddan tashqari ambitsiyali methodology ball olib, ijroda zarar keltirishi mumkin.

### MPC barcha ADB tenderlarida bir xilmi?

Yo'q. Evaluation modeli procurement documentsga bog'liq. 2026 tender package o'qilishi kerak, umumiy shablon emas.

### Kim muhimroq: yuristlarmi yoki injenerlar?

Ikkalasi ham. MPC texnik ishonchlilikni baholaydi, lekin Particular Conditions ichidagi contractual risk commercial natijani buzishi mumkin.
