Green Book ko'pincha loyiha "kichik" bo'lgani uchun tanlanadi. Bu xavfli mezon. Kichik contract texnik jihatdan oddiy bo'lishi mumkin, yoki katta disputega kichik eshik bo'lishi mumkin: noaniq scope, urgent mobilisation, boshqa tomon designi, live-site interfaces, payment uncertainty va yomon control qilingan changes.

FIDIC Green Book 2021 - Short Form of Contract second edition. Uning jozibasi aniq: kamroq procedure, qisqa structure, tezroq documentation. Lekin u faqat loyiha haqiqatan short formga mos bo'lganda yaxshi ishlaydi.

## Short Form "soddalashtirilgan Red Book" emas

Green Bookni Red Bookdan sahifalar olib tashlangan variant deb o'ylamang. Bu boshqa management model.

Red Book Employer-designed works, measurement va Engineer administration kuchli bo'lgan holatga mos. Yellow Book design-build uchun. Silver Book EPC/turnkey risk allocation uchun.

Green Book "administration arzon" bo'lgani uchun emas, loyiha qisqa procedure bilan controlni yo'qotmasligi uchun tanlanadi.

Umumiy tanlov uchun [Rainbow Series](/uz/knowledge/rainbow-guide) va [Book Selector](/uz/tools/book-selector)ga qarang.

## Green Book qachon mos

Short Form odatda bir nechta shartlar mos kelganda ishlaydi:

- scope awarddan oldin aniq;
- design information yetarlicha mature;
- boshqa contractors bilan interfaces kam;
- site access va permits murakkab critical dependency yaratmaydi;
- variations bo'lishi mumkin, lekin commercial modelni egallamaydi;
- dispute value og'ir contract machineryni oqlamaydi;
- tomonlar tez va intizomli administratsiya qila oladi.

Misollar: local works, small refurbishment, standalone engineering packages, simple supply-and-install, repair works yoki limited civil works package. Lekin har biri actual scope va risk allocationga bog'liq.

## Green Book qachon tuzoq bo'ladi

Short Form formalan kichik, lekin amalda murakkab loyihada yomon ishlaydi.

Birinchi red flag - **immature scope**. Employer nima qurilishini aniq bilmasa, short contract uncertainty ni arzon qilmaydi; uni claims stagegacha yashiradi.

Ikkinchi red flag - **complex interfaces**: operating facility ichida ishlash, boshqa contractor equipmentiga bog'liqlik, staged handover, utility relocation yoki external approvals.

Uchinchi red flag - **unclear design responsibility**. Contractor design solutions qilishi kutilib, documents buni "minor design" desa, responsibility dispute deyarli tayyor.

To'rtinchi red flag - **MDB funding va heavy compliance obligations**. Kichik package ham ESHS, procurement integrity, reporting va bank-specific requirements sabab murakkab bo'lishi mumkin. Qarang: [ESHS obligations](/uz/knowledge/eshs-obligations-mdb-fidic) va [World Bank Section VIII](/uz/knowledge/world-bank-section-viii-contract-data).

## Green Book tanlashdan oldin decision test

| Savol | Javob "yo'q" bo'lsa |
|---|---|
| Scope major assumptionssiz yoziladimi? | Green Book claims-heavy contract yaratishi mumkin |
| Price va measurement modeli aniqmi? | Payment architecture kuchaytirish kerak |
| Variations exception bo'ladimi? | Variation control kuchaytiriladi |
| Design responsibility aniqmi? | Yellow Book yoki special drafting ko'riladi |
| Site access va interfaces controlledmi? | Delay risk past baholanishi mumkin |
| Team short formda ham records yuritadimi? | "Simple contract" boshqarilmay qoladi |

Ikki-uch javob shubhali bo'lsa, Green Book avtomatik rad etilmaydi. Lekin u default emas, deliberate choice bo'lishi kerak.

## Green Book vs Red Book

Red Book odatda Employer design qilgan, quantities measured bo'lgan va Engineer role rivojlangan loyihalarda yaxshiroq.

Green Book yaxshiroq bo'lishi mumkin, agar:

- scope compact;
- Engineer role og'ir emas;
- payment va completion criteria oson tekshiriladi;
- change va delay risk limited;
- procedure cost ish qiymatidan oshmasligi kerak.

Loyiha faqat price bo'yicha kichik, complexity bo'yicha emas bo'lsa, Red Book halolroq form bo'lishi mumkin.

## Green Book vs Yellow Book

Contractor substantial design responsibility olsa, Yellow Book ko'rilishi kerak. Green Book limited design elements bilan ishlashi mumkin, lekin boundary aniq yozilishi kerak.

"Contractor shall complete all necessary design" kabi iboralar Employer's Requirements, design review, approvals va fitness-for-purpose standardsiz xavfli.

Design-build yoki EPCga yaqin loyihada [Silver Book va EPC/Turnkey](/uz/knowledge/silver-book-epc) bilan solishtirish foydali.

## FAQ

### Green Book 2021 faqat juda kichik projects uchunmi?

Yo'q. Size asosiy masala emas. Scope simplicity, risk manageability, price clarity va short procedure yetarliligi muhim.

### MDB-funded projectsda Green Book ishlatish mumkinmi?

Bu procurement documents, bank requirements va packagega bog'liq. Ruxsat bo'lsa ham, compliance, ESHS va reporting administrationni "short" qilmasligi mumkin.

### Eng katta xavf nima?

Short form riskni avtomatik kamaytiradi deb o'ylash. Scope xom, design responsibility noaniq yoki site constraints og'ir bo'lsa, risk yo'qolmaydi.

Bridge Consult Green Book 2021 packagega mosligini, Particular Conditions qayerda kerakligini, clarification questions va pricing risksni tekshirishga yordam beradi.
