World Bank loyihalarida contract award bilan tugamaydi. Aksincha, eng qimmat risk ko'pincha signaturedan keyin boshlanadi: weak mobilisation, unclear authority, delayed approvals, poorly managed variations, disputed payments va claims.

Contract Management Plan (CMP) post-award administration alohida odamlar xotirasiga bog'liq bo'lmasligi uchun kerak. Bu contract management uchun operational map.

## Contract Management Plan nima

CMP - contract awarddan keyin jamoa kontraktni qanday boshqarishini tushuntiradigan practical document.

FIDIC kontekstida u quyidagilarni bog'laydi:

- Employer, Engineer, Contractor va Contract Manager roles;
- communication procedures;
- approvals va delegations;
- time management;
- cost and payment control;
- quality, ES obligations va risk management;
- changes va variations;
- claims va dispute management;
- reporting and performance monitoring.

Bu formal legal appendix emas. Yaxshi CMP - project team ishlatadigan instrument.

## FIDIC uchun nega muhim

FIDIC procedural architecture beradi, lekin real loyiha operational discipline talab qiladi. Contract notice, determination, payment va variation proceduresni yozadi, lekin Employer ichida kim data yig'adi, kim documents tekshiradi, kim register yuritadi va internal review deadlinesni nazorat qiladi - buni har doim ko'rsatmaydi.

CMP shu bo'shliqni yopadi.

MDB loyihalarida bu ayniqsa muhim: decisions contractually correct va audit-ready bo'lishi kerak.

## CMPga nima kiradi

Minimal structure:

1. **Contract summary** - parties, form, price, Time for Completion, key dates.
2. **Governance** - delegated authorities, decision rights, escalation route.
3. **Communication protocol** - official channels, registers, meeting rhythm.
4. **Programme management** - baseline, updates, progress review, delay alerts.
5. **Cost and payment control** - IPC workflow, supporting documents, retention, advance recovery.
6. **Change control** - variation initiation, valuation, approvals, records.
7. **Claims management** - notice register, event register, particulars, determinations.
8. **Risk register** - owner, mitigation, early warning, review frequency.
9. **Quality and ES management** - compliance records and reporting.
10. **Dispute pathway** - Engineer's determination, DAAB, amicable settlement, arbitration.

CMP contractni takrorlamasligi kerak. U contractni daily managementga tarjima qiladi.

## Contract Manager va Engineer

Contract formga qarab contract management function Engineer, Employer's Representative, Project Manager yoki Employer internal teamda bo'lishi mumkin. FIDIC loyihalarida Engineer functioni xiralashmasligi kerak.

[Engineer role](/uz/knowledge/engineer-role) haqidagi maqola independence va procedural care nega muhimligini tushuntiradi. CMP bu rolni qo'llab-quvvatlashi kerak, har Engineer decisionni internal politicsga aylantirmasligi kerak.

Particular Conditions Employer approvals kiritishi mumkin. CMP buni halol yozishi kerak: qayerda Engineer qaror qiladi, qayerda internal approval kerak.

## Registers - post-award control skeleti

FIDIC loyiha uchun kamida:

- correspondence register;
- notices register;
- claims/events register;
- variation register;
- payment register;
- programme submissions register;
- design/RFI register;
- risk register;
- decision/determination register;
- dispute/DAAB register.

Registers bo'lmasa, loyiha inbox orqali boshqariladi. Bu missed deadlines va weak evidencega olib keladi.

## CMP va claims prevention

Yaxshi CMP nafaqat claims submit qilishga, balki ularning oldini olishga yordam beradi:

- access va approvals bo'yicha early warning;
- critical path regular review;
- design issues tez escalation;
- transparent variations workflow;
- clear payment workflow;
- events bo'yicha records discipline.

Jamoa failure ni qanchalik erta ko'rsa, uni dispute emas, contract administration darajasida hal qilish ehtimoli shuncha yuqori.

## FAQ

### CMP faqat Employer uchunmi?

Yo'q. Contractor uchun ham internal CMP foydali: notices kim yuboradi, records kim yuritadi, particulars kim tayyorlaydi, programme va cost data kim tekshiradi.

### CMP Particular Conditions o'rnini bosadimi?

Yo'q. CMP contractni o'zgartirmaydi. U contract qanday ijro qilinishini va kim nima uchun javobgarligini yozadi.

### CMP qachon tayyorlanadi?

Commencementdan oldin yoki mobilisation boshida. Birinchi katta claimdan keyin tayyorlansa, u reactive document bo'ladi.

Bridge Consult MDB-funded FIDIC loyihalari uchun Contract Management Plan tayyorlash va review qilishda yordam beradi: registers, payment workflow, claims protocol va dispute readiness.
